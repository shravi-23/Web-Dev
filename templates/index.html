PSLV-C60

The PSLV-C60 mission, launched by the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) on December 30, 2024, marked a significant milestone in India's space endeavors. This mission successfully deployed the Space Docking Experiment (SpaDeX), aiming to demonstrate in-space docking technology—a capability previously achieved only by the United States, Russia, and China.
